ENIGMA (Circuit Designing & Debugging)






General Guidelines:

1. The event will be held in three rounds.
-Technical Aptitude Test-25 questions (2 mark each).
From all the participant groups, 15 groups will be screened for the second round from this aptitude test.
-Circuit Debugging- Debugging on the CDS board.
All selected 15 groups will be provided with 1 circuit each already built on the CDS board. Each circuit will be provided with its name. It will contain some errors or false connections. They all are to be corrected within the given time.
From this round 7 groups will be screened out for final round.
-Circuit Designing & vive-voce.
- In this all the groups will be provided with a question on a blank page. They have to design the circuitry for the given question on the paper within provided amount of time.
- After this there will be viva on the design problem.
- On the basis of combined results of designing and viva, final three rankers will be announced.
2. Maximum 2 participants per team.
